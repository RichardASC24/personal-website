Portfolio

Mash Game

I developed a fun game of MASH (Mansion, Apartment, Shack, and House). I used arrays to store various options within each category. I designed a user component allowing players to input their decisions. Utilizing a random number generator, I implemented a cyclic elimination process to narrow the options. Eventually, the program displays the final selections to the user, providing a glimpse into their predicted future.

2D Arcade Game

I created a fun and exciting game where you control a character. Your goal is to collect coins and avoid other falling objects. Each coin you collect increases your score, but hitting other objects will decrease it. There are lively sound effects and background music to make the game more immersive. You can also press the spacebar to change your character's color, which adds a strategic twist to avoid collisions.
